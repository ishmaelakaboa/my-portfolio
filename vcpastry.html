Overview

eCommerce for
African brands

VCpastry is a custom-built eCommerce platform tailored for an African pastries brand. I architected and developed the entire solution — from product catalog management and shopping cart functionality to multi-auth vendor dashboards and payment integration with Paystack.

The platform supports a growing food brand with order management, inventory tracking, customer accounts, and a responsive, mobile-first design that ensures a smooth shopping experience on any device — critical for the African market where mobile dominates.

Built with Laravel's robust backend, MySQL for reliable data storage, and Tailwind CSS for a clean, conversion-focused UI. The Paystack integration handles both card and Mobile Money payments seamlessly.
